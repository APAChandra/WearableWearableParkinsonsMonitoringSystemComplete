Detailed Description

A helper class to provide methods to record audio input from the mic and its playback. Recording is supported to a file or to an OutputStream and playback is supported from a file or an InputStream. After constructing an instance of this class, you can call the appropriate methods to start or stop recording or playback. Callers can also provide listeners that would be notified when recording or playback tasks end, whether on their own or due to an exception or interruption. Note that for recording, caller should obtain

android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO

permission.
